ErgCalc Help

The pace calculator helps you convert between 500m split, time and distance. To use the pace calculator, enter two out of the three fields, and then click "Done" on the keyboard and the third will be processed and displayed.
The Predictions part of the app uses Paul's law to take a known test result and extrapolate from that what you would expect to get at a different distance. To use, enter the known test result, and enter the distance you would like to predict, then press "Done" on the keyboard.
The Weight Adjustment Calculator can be used to compare different athletes, attempting to control for differences in power-to-weight ratio. Enter your weight, and either a distance or a time, and the calculator will show your weight adjusted score.
